the process of handling all incidents throughout their lifecycle from when they are first reported to when they are either resolved or nothing more can be done in relation to them is known as incident as possible to minimize adverse impacts on business operations and ensure the best possible service quality and availability the primary objective of incident management is to standardize methods and procedures used for the efficient and prompt response of IT services incident management increases the visibility of incident communication to business and IT support staff in addition it aligns
