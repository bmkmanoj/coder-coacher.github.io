issue and change control procedure now let's go through the second procedure of configuration management strategy which is issue and change control procedure prince2 provides a common approach to dealing with requests for change of specifications and problems / concerns this procedure also consists of few steps and those are capture examine propose decide and implement the first step in the procedure is to capture the issue and undertake initial analysis to determine the type of issue that has been raised and whether it can be managed informally or formally issues being managed formally should be entered in the issue register and given a unique identifier an issue report should be created to capture what's already known about the issue it is often useful to ask the person who raised the issue to create the initial issue report the next step is to examine the issue by undertaking an impact analysis the impact analysis should consider the impact the issue has or will have on the project performance targets in terms of time cost quality and scope the project business case especially in terms of the impact on benefits the project risk profile ie the impact on the overall risk exposure of the project impact analysis must cover the three areas of business user and supplier for example the suppliers cost and effort required to implement a change and what products would have to be changed having undertaken the impact says the severity or priority should be re-evaluated it may be necessary to request advice from the project board to check their understanding of the issues priority or severity before proposing resolutions in the proposed step you need to consider alternative options or responding to it and proposing a course of action to take consideration should be given as to the effect of each of the options will have on the project's time cost quality scope benefit and risk performance targets remember there must be a balance between the advantage to be gained by implementing the option and the time cost and risk of implementing it if the proposed option would take the stage or project beyond any tolerances you as a project manager should consider preparing an exception report for that option to accompany the issue report the next step is decide in some situations the project manager may be able to resolve issues without the need to escalate them to the project board other issues may require escalation to the project board or its delegated change Authority for a decision the escalation could be in the form of an issue report or in the form of an exception report we will discuss the decision making process in detail in the next slide once again the last step in the issue and change control procedure is to implement depending on the project boards decision the project manager will take the necessary corrective action or create an exception plan for example updating a work packages to incorporate the agreed changes or issuing an all to get a new work package to take care of the change
