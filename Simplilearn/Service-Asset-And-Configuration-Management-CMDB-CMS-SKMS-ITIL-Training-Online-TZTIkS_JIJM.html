service knowledge management system or s kms is the complete set of integrated repositories or databases that are used to manage knowledge and information the s kms includes the configuration management system as well as other tools and databases There s km s stores manages updates and presents all information that an IT service provider needs to manage the full lifecycle of its services the main purpose of the s kms is to provide quality information so that informed decisions can be made by the IT service provider CMS or configuration management system is a set of tools and databases that are used to manage an IT service providers configuration data the CMS also includes information about incidents problems known errors changes and releases and may contain data about employees suppliers locations business unit customers and users the CMS is maintained by service asset and configuration management and is used by all IT service management processes two major components of CMS are CMDB or configuration management database it stores configuration details of the IT infrastructure ke DB or known error database the database is created by problem management and is used by incident and problem management however the s km s is a broader concept that covers a much wider base of knowledge for example the experience of staff records of peripheral matters for example user numbers and behavior organization's performance figures suppliers and partners requirements abilities and expectations typical and anticipated user skill levels and so on this brings us to the end of Service asset and configuration management process in the next few slides of service transition module we will discuss release and deployment management process
