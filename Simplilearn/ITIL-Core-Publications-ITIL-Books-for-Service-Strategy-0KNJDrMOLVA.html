ITIL Core Publications | ITIL Books for Service Strategy

2011-11-29
the ITIL core library is composed of five books or publications representing each of the service lifecycle stages the first book on service strategy focuses on design development and implementation of service management so that the IT services are able to provide value to business service strategy basically helps an organization identify what IT services should be offered and to whom how do we differentiate ourselves from competing alternatives how do we create value for our customers the second book on service design provides guidance for the design and the development of services and processes covering design principles and methods for converting objectives of IT organizations into portfolios of services and service assets so the service design basically deals with design of new and changed services that an IT organization wants to offer to its customers the third book on service transition covers the development and improvement of capabilities for transitioning new and change services into the live production or operating environment so the service transition ensures that a new services or a change to an existing services is properly tested and deployed into the live production environment the fourth book on service operation takes a look at the different practices in the management of service operation and includes guidance on achieving efficiency and effectiveness in the delivery and support of services meaning service operation takes of coordinating and carrying out day-to-day activities and processes to deliver and managed IT services the fifth book on continual service improvement is focused on creating and maintaining value for customers through better design introduction and operation of services the aim here is to continually align IT services to changing business needs by identifying and implementing improvement so in other words the aim is to continuously look for ways to improve efficient and cost effectiveness as part of ITIL version 3 foundation course one module is dedicated to each one of these ITIL core books so we have module 2 on service strategy module 3 on service design and so on knowing about these five books is our entire syllabus of ITIL v3 foundation course
