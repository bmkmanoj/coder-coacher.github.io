Android Developer Story: Touchten - Finding success in a local market

2017-11-10
The partners at TouchTen we are brothers that really enjoy games ever since we were young. And so naturally when we come form a company, we want to work on something we really enjoy and that is mobile games. Mobile games is a global competitive market. But coming back to the local market, we see strong support from the Indonesian and Southeast Asian gamers towards local content. The strength that we know we have is that we are local, we are here, we speak the language and we know the culture. The games that we make have a lot of things in common which is food. And this is a really good inspiration to make this game, Warung Chain. Part of the insight that we also get is that a lot of foodies, people who like food, also like to be able to own a shop. Our game gives that kind of simulation where you own and manage a restaurant. Now for Warung Chain close to 3 million people download the game. What Google Play has allowed us to do What Google Play has allowed us to do is to very easily launch our games bring it to the gamers that like it to gain insights and improvements on the game as a result. For one of our games called Dessert Chain we just did AB testing for the app icon. More than 60% of our local users prefer character icon to food icon. That’s super awesome feedback for us. We were very proud to submit Warung Chain in Indonesia Game contest the very first local game competition provided by Google. We got really really hyped when the Google Play team said that Warung Chain is the winner. This is home to many vibrant local developers as well as the young population that we have which I think will be a market not to be ignored. In the same way that we try to understand our local audience we want to bring this to the global audience and to bring meaningful content to them. This goes in line with our company name Touch Ten is to touch the sky and that’s our aim. We are very proud to be made in Indonesia and we want to put Indonesia in the map a game creator, not just as a game consumer.
