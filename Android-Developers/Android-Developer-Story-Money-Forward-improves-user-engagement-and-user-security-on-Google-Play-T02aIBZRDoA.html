Android Developer Story: Money Forward improves user engagement and user security on Google Play

2016-08-03
We all have apprehensions when it comes to money. So I thought, why not use technology to help with that? Money Forward lets you manage your financial information from bank accounts to credit cards, bonds, and loyalty points all from one app. With just a glance at your smartphone, you can check everything instantly your income, your outgoings, your current balance, and so on. All of which are updated in real time. We inquired about Google's Material Design as soon as it was announced and it's been really beneficial to us. Within the first month, we experienced a 30% increase in total users a 7% increase in user retention and a 4% decrease in bounce rate. Using the UI templates on offer we were able to create a great UI that works for our product. We've found that tablet users are 1.5 times more likely to pay for in-app purchases than smartphone users. While users who use both smartphones and tablets are around three times as likely. Before using Material Design we had to set aside a lot of time whenever there was an OS update. But now, the Android Support Library guarantees that even after an OS update the app will behave the same. It's a real time saver. CoordinatorLayout is a great example of how it has helped. There was one case where we'd developed a feature to automatically hide the header when scrolling down a page. But this stopped working after an OS upgrade. Fortunately, this kind of function is now covered by the Support Library. Our app will soon support Android Wear. You can now add to your account book using voice recognition. We're also thinking of adding a feature to notify users about their incoming debits. For example, "Payment due on Monday." or, "Your balance is insufficient." Given that our service handles sensitive customer information we are very mindful of security. This means operating strict security measures at every step from handling device data to encrypting transmissions. Google gives us the perfect platform to explore new technology and create great applications that we hope will have a positive impact on many people's lives.
