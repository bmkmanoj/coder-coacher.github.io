Android Developer Story: Framy improves user experience with Material Design

2016-06-08
Framy stimulates creativity by allowing us to create animations and characters using a wealth of real facial expressions By clicking just few buttons Framy can create movie-like effects integrating and combining photos with video clips However, Framy is not just a tool for producing videos it is also a platform to express yourself and your emotions It’s a great channel to share your custom animations and exchange stories from your imagination with people all over the world. Google has a wealth of experience and tools to help you build a successful app business Google Play provided Framy with a lot of useful ideas and advice. For example, Google Play recommends adopting Material Design with enabled Framy to generate a new design scheme and a simple and unified workflow. In the creation of Framy The developers also incorporated Various services provided by Google Such as Google stage rollout Framy is an app that contained a lot of new innovative features Through Google Staged Rollout. Framy can introduce new features to a limited set of users And according to their report to revise the problem and improve before releasing them to the public Staged Rollouts is especially useful for Framy in designing new experimental features Framy also leveraged Google Cloud Test Lab. It enables Framy to test on multiple devices which saves a lot of testing manpower and time. We can then make system adjustments according to device types therefore greatly improving user satisfaction with our app performance Amongst various Google features Framy also adopted Google Translate Which provided different language support and minimal language barriers on Framy’s social media platform. This resulted in a massive boost to our user ratings which increased from 3.5 to 4.1 and the number is still improving. In the future, Framy will continue to improve and incorporate relevant Google services into its workflow in order to make Framy a unique, and user-friendly app that everyone can enjoy.
