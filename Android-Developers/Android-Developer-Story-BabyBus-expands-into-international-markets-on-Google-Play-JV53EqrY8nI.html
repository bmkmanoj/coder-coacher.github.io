Android Developer Story: BabyBus expands into international markets on Google Play

2016-10-14
My name is Tang Guangyu, founder and CEO of BabyBus Everyone calls me "Donald Duck” and I’m hoping this amusing nickname brings more happiness to all the children As a father I used to watch my kids playing and having fun so that I have to keep finding new ways for them to play Our team has many young parents and education experts together we are trying to determine the most suitable and enjoyable educational app for each and every kid across different age groups We hope that our “bus” get the same magical power to travel around the world and to deliver surprises and happiness to every child like Santa Claus Now, BabyBus's “Magic” just got a lot stronger with Google Play’s firm supports My name is Louis Co-founder and App Manager of BabyBus Currently, 80% of our international users are using Android Starting from September 2015 all of our apps joined the “Designed for Families” section so that kids and parents can easily find the app based on their interest Our downloads has increased by 367% comparing with last year “Ice Cream &amp; Smoothies” is our most popular app on Google Play it was officially promoted on Google Play homepage During that week, it gained more than 1.36 million downloads and our other apps received more than 6.12 million downloads altogether My name is Ling Ying, Product Manager of BabyBus We pay a lot of attention to our overseas users we have created 12 different languages versions for most of our apps which allows them to meet local user's preferences Thanks to Google Play App Translation Service our users are able to read content in their native language Admob is the main method we use for app monetization it provides more than 90% of total income from oversea markets Cooperating with Google Play is the key to our success in oversea markets We released our first app on Google Play in 2012 now we have 103 apps in the market at the same time with the number of total downloads exceeding 150 million With Google Play’s support we believe we will continue to bring great educational products for children around the world
