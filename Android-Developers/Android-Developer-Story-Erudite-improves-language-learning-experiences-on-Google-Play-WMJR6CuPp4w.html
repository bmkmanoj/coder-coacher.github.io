Android Developer Story: Erudite improves language learning experiences on Google Play

2016-10-14
I’m Neutron, Developer of Erudite Erudite is a mobile language learning app integrated with all essential tools including dictionaries, phrasebooks, flashcards and supports Android Wear so users can learn English anywhere anytime with their native tongue Users who speaks English can also learn 24 foreign languages with Erudite It’s our approach to break the language barrier and bridge the gap between different cultures We took Google's suggestion and updated the app with Material Design This made our user rating in Google Play increased from 4.1 to 4.3 Erudite now has over 1M downloads globally with users from more than 200 countries It has been selected as the Editor's Choice and App of Year 2015 on Google Play All these acknowledgements also encouraged us a lot My name is Benji, Product Manager of Erudite Maintaining user retention is our key priority We implemented Pre-Launch Report to collect data from a variety of Android devices so we can reduce as many bugs as possible before its official release This helps us to improve user satisfaction and our user retention rate has increased by over 60% Firebase enables us to monitor analytics crash reporting, and other app-specific data including location, age, gender, device type, etc. Firebase enables us to know our users from different aspects and hence make quicker decisions and better app Additionally, we use Review Analysis to gather feedback from users on Google Play and use them to polish Erudite's features It also allows us to reply to users directly and individually so we can improve the communication with our users and know their true requirements Google is the bridge connecting Erudite and its users from all around the world I’m Winson, Erudite’s Technical Manager Google’s great support all along the way makes us more confident about the future We are trying to build a crowdsource platform to enable users to contribute learning materials to each other and providing our users with richer language learning experiences
