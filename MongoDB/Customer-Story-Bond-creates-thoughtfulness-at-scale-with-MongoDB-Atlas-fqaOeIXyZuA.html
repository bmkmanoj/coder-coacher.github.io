Customer Story: Bond creates thoughtfulness at scale with MongoDB Atlas

2017-04-04
in today's day and age of automated marketing social media banner ads and direct mail the personal touch is more important than ever my name is Sam bro I'm the head of product and technology at bond for the last three years we've been creating digital products that enable our customers to send beautiful thoughtful handwritten correspondence from their computer or phone we knew that our product was taking off when we started receiving tens of thousands of note orders and had to quickly figure out how to keep up with that demand we believe that most companies want to do really nice personal things for their customers but the real reason people don't or the businesses don't is because it's a scale problem what we were out solving a scale problem for our customers we actually ran into a scale problem of our own when we initially built our product we built it on a relational database we ran into an issue where we were getting so much data that we weren't able to ensure consistent writes because of how many reads we were doing it became clear that we were running into scale issues when our team was focused more on our infrastructure than they were on building the product we investigated a handful of options for our new database some required too much infrastructure some didn't have enough availability but MongoDB fit right into our scope what was important to us was consistency of data and availability without having to worry about partitioning one of the things that concerned me when we began talking about switching from a relational database to a no sequel database was the complex queries I knew we had to run for our reporting especially across collections fortunately MongoDB is aggregation framework allowed us the same functionality once we made the switch to MongoDB we were able to double the number of messages we sent in only two months from the number we had sent the entire year before we wanted our developers focused on building out our products and not messing around with infrastructure we decided to go with a third party hosting solution but we began running into problems with encryption and flexibility the third party vendors we were using for our MongoDB instances didn't give us the flexibility to tune our settings exactly to our needs we heard about MongoDB Atlas pretty much right away after it was launched and we decided to build a prototype do a proof-of-concept and see how it worked we loved the results and we almost immediately began switching our infrastructure over to MongoDB Atlas MongoDB is not only helping us solve scale problems with our customer facing products but it's also helping us solve fulfillment scale problems that we have with our robotic fleet and our internal filmin system now that we've switched a MongoDB Atlas we're in a much better place to be able to meet the demands of our customers at bond we believe everybody wants to be more thoughtful and that we can create the technology and the tools that make that easier it helps people communicate better it helps people be nicer
