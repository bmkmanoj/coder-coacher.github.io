companies can be large or small they can be in different fields manufacturing agriculture transportation education finance no two companies are the same but there are a couple of things that can be said about any company first every company has customers with a problem to be solved even animal shelters and government agencies have customers maybe you work on a team that works only with internal customers other company team's second companies solve problems by providing some kind of product or service a cosmetics company makes hair products to solve the problem of bed hair a beverage company makes an energy drink to combat tiredness an education company creates online courses to close the skills gap a tech company creates a game engine for mobile games to eliminate boredom companies identify problems come up with solutions implement their ideas and then deliver products to customers so you can think of it as a cycle that starts and ends with the customer the challenge is to build a high-quality cost-effective and reliable solution that you can put in the hands of your customer as soon as possible it's important to be in alignment on your overall company goal when development teams are rewarded for launching new features quickly but operations is rewarded for keeping things stable you have the potential for conflict across teams when you realize that the real goal is to deliver good service to your customers teams are more likely to work together to find a solution to do this you have to understand your customers are you working for external or internal customers if you are a part of a large organization who are the internal people who use your work what is the main problem that your company or division is trying to solve
