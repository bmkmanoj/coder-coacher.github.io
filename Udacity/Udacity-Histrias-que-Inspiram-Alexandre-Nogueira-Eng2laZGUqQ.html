Udacity - Histórias que Inspiram - Alexandre Nogueira

2017-11-23
Today I came to work by car. In the future, this will surely be very different. I’ll be brought to work by a self-driving car. My name is Alexandre Nogueira. I am a Computer Engineer, and study Self-Driving Car Engineer Nanodegree at Udacity. My first job was at Ericsson and I’ve been working there ever since. I worked with system development and project management, and lately with new business development. After many years, I was working at Ericsson as a consultant, and I became a father to two little girls, and my life changed quite a bit. When they grew up, I discovered I had a lot of time on my hands. I decided to fill this time by going back to school. I was very interested in Udacity’s Self-Driving Car program, and I thought that it was time to have a technical understanding of how self-driving cars work to be part of this revolution. I haven’t stopped studying since and have discovered that studying is a great career booster. The knowledge I develop here with Udacity’s Nanodegree allows me to help the company develop new businesses in other emerging areas. I am also learning about new possibilities: What can we provide the market that is definitely technically grounded? When you understand something that is very advanced and complex, it allows you to work with simpler things more easily. Self-driving cars will bring more ease, they will provide new services, and most likely, they will give rise to new markets that we can’t even begin to imagine right now. With more efficiency and simplicity. This will surely improve people’s quality of life.
