Encryption Basics

2015-07-06
we often call an encryption scheme a cipher you may not have realized this but encryption has been used for thousands of years for example there is evidence that ancient Egyptians used some sort of ciphers and then there is the famous Caesar cipher as well as other similar schemes there are several types of ciphers symmetric ciphers range from asian schemes to present day algorithms asymmetric ciphers are relatively new only invented in the late 70s most security protocols now use both types of schemes typically they first use asymmetric ciphers to authenticate both parties involved in the communication and then establish an exchange in encryption key for their communication then they use symmetric ciphers and The Ecstatic encryption key to encrypt data and traffic they can also use asymmetric ciphers to digitally sign the data so that they can ensure data authenticity
