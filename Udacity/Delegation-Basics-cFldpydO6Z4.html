Delegation Basics

2016-06-06
a common answer we've heard from our students when we asked them about the word delegate is that it made him think of a relationship between a manager and an employee or a parent and a child perhaps you've also experienced the situation where work has been passed on or delegated to you in iOS delegation works much the same way but it can be between any types of objects it really just means getting one object to the work for the other in the case of av audio recorder it does not know anything about our view controllers or even your app it does know that once it finishes recording it can inform the other class our record view controller that it's finished the delegate the record view controller can then do the work of whatever needs to be done with the recorded audio in this way you can use AV audio recorder as a tool to just record the audio and it calls out to its delegate the record view controller to do something with it
