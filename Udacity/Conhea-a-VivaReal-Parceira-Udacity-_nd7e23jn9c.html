Meet Viva Real A partner company of Udacity I think everyone knows why Viva Real exists, because finding a property, renting and buying a property is hard. We see a great potential to organize this process, give people more information and assure people they can make a good decision about where to live. Today we are 600 people, we've grown a lot, and today we have over 5 million properties registered for rent and for sale. And I think that companies that forget they need to constantly review their existing ideas and bring new things will fail in the digital world. We truly believe in bringing new people, new talent, people who bring new ideas and think outside the box. Professionals who are prepared, those who are experienced, who have technical background they are very disputed in the market. So how will your company, how will Vival Real become a company which is irresistible enough so that they will refuse other offers and choose us? Today our greatest trouble is finding professionals who adhere to our culture. Who can identify with the way we work, with what we look for in terms of behaviour... Our team is arranged by products cell. We use effective methodologies but we don't use one specific methodology, we truly believe each team can work independently and we standardize a few things. We standardize the interfaces accross teams So the way requests get to the teams, the way we document each product's needs, and the way these teams shoud communicate. But the internal processes are determined by each team. Usually, by giving them space we reap much more benefits than by creating something very formal. It's one of the few places I've worked where I feel truly comfortable. Which motivates me the most here besides the freedom we have within the Viva Real platform are the people. So here we have a team that is very good and very diverse. You end up learning a lot each day. Learning is essential for a tech professional. Those who are not interested in keeping themselves updated will get quickly outdated. It even becomes a matter of survival. Especially when you wish to work at cutting-edge companies. I believe our partnership with Udacity can help Viva Real find the best talent. We look for people who are always willing to get better and Udacity's students are already doing that. We often have positions opened in data so data analystics and data science are openings we very often have. An expertise area we've been always looking at is performance marketing. Today I have openings for developers in our back teams when we think about the stacks of ruby, python... ...all you can imagine, because we do not limit the use of any technology. Front development.
