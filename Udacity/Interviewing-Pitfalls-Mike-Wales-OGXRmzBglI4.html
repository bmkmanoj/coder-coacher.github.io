Interviewing Pitfalls: Mike Wales

2016-06-06
I'm Mike wills and this is my bad interview experience so during an interview one time I was given 15 minutes to write the computer side of tic-tac-toe function that would play the computer side now I play tic-tac-toe a lot with my kids and we all know if you get three corners you win the game so that's what I approached it as and I'm standing at the whiteboard and I keep mumbling to myself just get three corners and I win I'm telling the the interviewer if I get three corners I win and I wasted 15 minutes trying to win the game of tic-tac-toe and at the end I didn't even have a function that would play the game of tic-tac-toe it was horrible it wouldn't even run it would have been better for me to just write a function that would put an X in the very first available slot instead I'm too worried about winning and winning and winning I walked away from that I had a friend who interviewed at the same company and I was like dude I had this question and I didn't do very well and he laughed about it and he reassured me he said I bombed it as well the first time and then we sat down and worked through another solution that not only played the game but also played it very well and so that was fun and I got to learn a little bit from the process as well
