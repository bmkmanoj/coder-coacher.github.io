Learning Zones

2016-06-06
when I started learning how to code I use the concept of learning zones to help me map out my new journey as a new programmer I always reflected on the skills I was learning and tracked whether i was in my safe zone where concepts are familiar and where we don't need to take any risks stretch zone where we were introduced to new concepts and we're making new discoveries or strain zone where learning becomes difficult and is often blocked by a sense of frustration the border between the stretch and the strain zone is the points of maximum learning our goal is to keep you between your stretch and your strain zone so you are always learning whenever you learn something new you may feel like you're learning zones are fluctuating one moment you could be in your safe zone and the next minute you're in your strain zone this should be totally expected learning something new especially programming can be a winding journey with many highs and lows let's read a little bit more about your learning zones
