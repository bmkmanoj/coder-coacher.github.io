Intrusion Detection System (IDS)

2016-06-06
intrusion detection systems or ideas for short are designed to counter these types of threats they can be quite effective against known or less sophisticated attacks such as large-scale email phishing attacks however as attack techniques become more sophisticated ideas will become less effective for example attackers can blend attack traffic with normal activities so that it is very hard to detect such attacks and the most sophisticated attackers in particular the state sponsored hackers may use new or zero-day exploits to render ideas completely ineffective since ideas are not always effective they need to be part of the defense in depth strategy for an organization typical defense-in-depth strategies should include encrypting scientific information provide detailed audit trails of activities on systems and networks use strong authentication and access control actively manage the security of operating system and applications
