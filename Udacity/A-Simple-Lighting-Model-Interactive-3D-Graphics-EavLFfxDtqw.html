some constant color regardless of the lighting conditions the second component ambient is a fudge factor something added in so objects look better especially in areas that are not directly light ambient term remember that we made a simplification with lights in a scene we assume photons come only from lights and don't bounce around in reality photons would reflect from the floor to the bottom
