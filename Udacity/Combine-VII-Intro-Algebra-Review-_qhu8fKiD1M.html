Combine VII - Intro Algebra Review

2015-02-23
5x minus 7 minus 4x simplifies to 9x minus 7 this is a tricky one in this problem it is very helpful to rewrite 5x minus 7 minus 4x as 5x plus negative 1 times 7 plus negative 4x I know this looks a little messy but this will help us keep track of all the negative signs so this negative one needs to be distributed to both terms inside the parentheses and what we get is 5x plus negative 1 times 7 plus negative 1 times negative 4x simplifying further and removing the outer parentheses because there's nothing left to distribute we get 5x plus negative 7 plus 4x remember negative times a negative gives a positive these are our like terms which we can combine to get 9x then since seven can't combine with anything else we leave it how it is and our answer is 9x minus 7
