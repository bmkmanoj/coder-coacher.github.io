Conheça a 99 | Parceira Udacity

2017-05-25
Meet 99 A Partner Company of Udacity 99 was born as an app designed to link people to taxi drivers. Along the years, our goal is to have an increasing portfolio of products and offer all kinds of transport people use within cities. What are the professional profiles that we look for? Passionate people who believe they can make a difference in the world. So they have a working purpose, brightness in the eyes, willingness to do things, ownership, which we fondly call 99rship. We truly believe we got where we did and achieved the success we have thanks to the people we managed to bring to 99, the team we built. With almost all our candidates we do some kind of test, something to get to know them better. We do a cultural assessment, since we have a strong culture at the company. My journey here at 99 was real fast in fact, in terms of responsibility and learning. I believe this is a very usual attribute among these startups that begin with a project that has value, that has a purpose. And attracts very good people at first, people that are interested. And things change very quickly, overnight. The greatest challenge is having a lot to do. The amount of things is huge! You usually work at a very lean team, so you have to be careful on choosing what to do. And you also need to do a lot of different things. You do not specialize in a single system, you deal with a whole ecosystem of different technologies. At least here at 99 we have little red tape. You don't go through lots of layers for approval, things you need to do for launching a new code. And something I enjoy doing is following the result of things. We are arranged it in a way that is famous for being brought by Spotify, which is called squads. Squads are multidisciplinary teams, which is, inside the same team there is one person who delevops iOS, one who develops Android, one responsible for the server part, someone from product, someone from Marketing. We see Udacity as a great potential partner. Professionals really prove to have lots of qualities. Among them, there is one we find very valuable, which is the continuous and endless search for knowledge. The capabilities we look for in a person are Java, Scala is a plus, or native Android, native iOS, experience with Amazon AWS, knowledge of agile methodologies, and experience working at a large-scale app. Get ready for the market
