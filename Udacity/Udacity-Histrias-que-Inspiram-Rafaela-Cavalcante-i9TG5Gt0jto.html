Udacity - Histórias que Inspiram - Rafaela Cavalcante

2017-12-12
Technology came along as a way to allow me to achieve a balance and to help me get things done. I am Rafaela. I'm from Fortaleza. I attended a Management School. I've worked at multinationals, mainly in Sales and Marketing. I had never considered learning programming. I've always liked logic, and I've always worked with MS Excel. I was always that person searching through the menus, finding new formulas, looking for more practical ways to do things. After working over 8 years for several companies, I made a decision to start studying Technology seriously, coming along in large part right now because I'm pregnant, because of the prospect of becoming a mother. That was when I started thinking I needed to follow a new path so I could have a balanced life the way I believe I should have. That's what I found at Udacity. Because it is online, it's easier. Being able to study at home is great. After I took the Intro to Programming course, my Tech studies are not something I would consider temporary. It's something you get interested in and study more thoroughly. Today I'm part of a network of motherhood entrepreneurship that helps women who want to tread new paths so they can discover a balance between family and work. Motherhood has a great impact on a person's life and family. Many women stop working because they aren't able to redefine themselves. But when you apply technology to it, new opportunities spring forth and these women can assume roles in the market, where they held few positions before. It's like a switch flicked on inside me. I can start programming. And I think that's empowering.
