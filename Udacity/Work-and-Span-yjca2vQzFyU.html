Given a dag like this one, I will always ask you two questions about it. The first questions is, how many vertices does it have in total? And I'm going to give this a name. Now computing the work is easy, you just count the number of vertices. The second thing I'll ask you about a dag always, is how long is the longest path through the dag? This also has a special name, I'm going to call it the span. And I'm going to use the symbol D of n to represent the span. Now in this dag, here is the longest path, which I've circled, and this path also has a special name. It's called the critical path. Now as a historical aside, the length of this critical path or what I just called the span, historically was called depth. And that's why I'm using the symbol D of n.
