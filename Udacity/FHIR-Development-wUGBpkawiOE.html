FHIR Development

2016-06-06
I've presented a very basic introduction to fire which is still very much in development and testing the standard encompasses many other issues including what values term value sets are allowed to define structured data elements within resources I encourage you to explore further and stay up to date as the standard develops recently for example an article appeared proposing three new fire resources to represent patients genomic data according to graham grieve a key challenge facing the fire standards development effort is achieving the right balance between functionality and complexity fire is not intended to represent all of healthcare rather it is intended to contain the key resources and details needed to represent the most common elements of health care I've served on standard development teams and it is entirely predictable that people whose favorite use case isn't perfectly covered will push for inclusion of more and more detail doing so clearly risks creating the very complexity that led to the idea of fire in the first place
