Requirements Prioritization - Georgia Tech - Software Development Process

2015-02-23
while collecting modeling in analyzing requirements we may realize that the resources available for the project are not enough to satisfy all of them for example there's not enough time not enough money not enough manpower and therefore there are some requirements that we won't be able to satisfy in these cases we must prioritize our requirements by classifying them in one of three classes the first class is mandatory requirements and these are the requirement that we must satisfy then there are the nice to have requirements that are the ones that we will satisfy if resources allow and finally there are the superfluous requirements and those are the requirements that we're going to keep around but that we're going to postpone for example we might decide to satisfy them in the next release
