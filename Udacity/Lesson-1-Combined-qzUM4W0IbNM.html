the traditional approach to supporting different devices is to make an app for each device and platform as they become available and adopted by users this is typically meant a new project for each and every device developed and supported independently for most of us we actually think of the experience on any of these devices as part of a single whole experience for the user with our service approaching each as a separate project as well it's just the wrong way to go about it instead you're going to want to think about having a view into your service from every device that makes sense this is really the definition of ubiquitous computing where your service is available anywhere and everywhere each view into your service will differ slightly depending on the features available and what the user needs in that context but taken as a whole these views lead to a single seamless experience for the user across their life
