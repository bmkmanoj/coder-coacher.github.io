Overdraw

2016-06-06
as a developer your goal is to keep your app rendering at 60 frames per second this means that you have 16 milliseconds to finish all of your computation and drawing for the next frame if you miss this 16 millisecond target you'll have a dropped frame which will cause your app to skip so you'll want to use this time wisely a common perf problem you've probably encountered is over draw the idea here is that you draw a layer great but then the next layer you draw obscures a portion of that initial layer you just wasted precious time drawing all of those pixels that were drawn over immediately after there are two ways to tackle over draw issues the first is to remove unnecessary backgrounds and drawables such as a base white background on your topmost layer when you're completely obscuring the layout with other views or you can mark certain portions of the layer as visible or not visible especially with stacked views this will help by only drawing the visible portions and ignoring the rest until it's needed
