ERP: Renaming Billing Records

2017-10-03
this video demonstrates how to rename records to match the terminology used by your business in this video we will focus on records related to advanced subscription billing but the same process applies to any records which allow renaming the first step in renaming records requires you to open the rename records transactions page do this by going to setup company and rename records transactions on the rename records transaction page locate the three subscription related records subscription subscription plan and subscription change order to rename these records simply enter the new name in the appropriate field in the name for subscription field will change subscription to contract and do the same in the name for subscription plan fields and in the name for subscription change order field now that all the subscription records are renamed click Save notice that in the UI navigation subscription has been replaced with contracts refer to the online help for renaming records to see where renaming occurs and where it doesn't for example while the label changes in the navigation the rename does not affect the enable features page nor will it appear in any error messages thank you for watching
