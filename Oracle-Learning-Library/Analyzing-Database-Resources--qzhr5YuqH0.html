Analyzing Database Resources

2017-06-02
[Music playing] [wind whooshing] Capacity planning ensures that your enterprise databases have sufficient resources to meet future business needs. As an IT Administrator or Capacity Planner you can use Resource Analytics for databases to understand how your critical resources, including CPU and storage, are used. By default, all databases are selected but you can select specific databases, or a group of databases, for which you want to view resource usage. For example, you can select all your production databases. The database selection applies to all reports within the application. The selected time period for which you want to view data, either by choosing one of the predefined time periods, or by specifying a custom time period. The time period selection applies to all reports within the application. Clicking the tabs in the navigation pane direct you to utilization information specific to the corresponding resource. For example, clicking the CPU tab loads CPU utilization data. The first chart shows the trend of CPU resource utilization. From this chart, you can also view your available CPU capacity. From the second chart, you can view the CPU utilization for each database, along with changes in CPU utilization during the specified time period. Each cell represents a database. The size and color represent the amount of resources used and the growth of the database. Red indicates a growth in resource usage while green indicates a reduction in the resources used by a particular database. You can also choose to view only the top 10 consumers of your CPU resources. By default, your databases are grouped by version. Alternatively, you can group the databases by other measures such as host or life cycle status. You can drill down further and view the trend of current resource utilization for the selected databases, and use this to generate a forecast of expected future usage. You'll have to select a time period of at least six months for the forecast feature to work. You can also select different algorithms to generate the best fit for the resource utilization. We hope this video helps you understand how Oracle IT Analytics Cloud Service helps you analyze current resource utilization and plan for future business growth. Thanks for watching
