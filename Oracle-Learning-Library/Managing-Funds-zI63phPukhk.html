Managing Funds

use the funds module to track funding availability and consumption in your projects manage money from a variety of sources such as grants endowments and government and maintain records to verify compliance with funding regulations in this video we'll explore how to create a fund allocated to projects and monitor its consumption use the funds page to add distributes and monitor funds funds can be created at the workspace or project level create a fund at the workspace level and then distribute money to projects funds are typically targeted at specific types of projects for example a capital replacement fund is used to update old equipment money and funds created at the workspace level can be allocated to multiple projects when allocating them designate how much of the money they can receive at the project level use the funds page to enter how much of the money is appropriated and how much of it has been consumed consumption can be added manually or rolled up automatically from actuals tracked on the project at the workspace level monitor how funds are being used across all projects and workspaces click the dollar amounts to view more detailed information for example on the parks maintenance relocation project click on the consumed link to view how much money has been spent from each funding source allocated to the project
