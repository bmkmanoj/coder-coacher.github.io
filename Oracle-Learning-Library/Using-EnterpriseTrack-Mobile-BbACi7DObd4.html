Using EnterpriseTrack Mobile

2017-01-31
after downloading the instances enterprise track mobile app from the itunes app store or google play store first agree to the terms and conditions at the login screen using your device scan the QR code from your browser based in status account to quickly populate the URL for your instance enter your username and password and sign-in this screen shows a list of your in progress activities drill down on an activity to see more details and even complete the activity swipe left to access the main menu for the app depending on your access to instances functionality the main menu can have links to activities projects dashboard views and timesheet approvals click on the project's link to see a list of your current project activity drill down on a project to see a summary of that project details such as percent complete high-level dates financial summary and counts of issues and risks drilling down on issues shows you the open issues for the project select an issue to review its status return to the main menu for additional options selecting dashboards shows a list of dashboards enabled for mobile viewing the timesheets link takes a resource or project manager to those timesheets awaiting their approval drill down on the users timesheet to see their activities and mark the time as approved or rejected return to the main menu select about to review further information on the app and to sign out
