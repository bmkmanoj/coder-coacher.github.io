Capital Plan Management

2014-03-31
capital plan management helps an organization hand serve which projects are the greatest value what are the budgetary constraints which projects should be funded and when and which projects should be deferred the capital plan management process to be divided into four phases plan select execute and monitor the plan select and monitor phases are executed in Oracle primavera prime the execute phase is done the integration with Primavera p6 first in the plan phase manage your project inventory by creating projects and porting them from p6 or proposing them through workflows create capital portfolios create capital plans and establish key performance indicators next in the select phase do an evaluation of the capital plans the form what is modeling and create scenarios estimate the budgets and finally select a capital plan that best meets the organization needs and then route it for approval next in the execute phase manage project schedules resources and costs be integration with Primavera p6 finally in the monitor phase analyze the health of the capital plan and then collaborate with stakeholders and adjust a capital plan
