Oracle Social Network: Managing Follow Ups and Favorites (Release 8)

2013-08-12
welcome to the Oracle social network video series this video covers signing and managing follow-ups and marking items as favorites Oracle social networks follow-up feature provides a way to bring someone's attention to a message or document the people you assigned to follow up are flagged in the application and depending on their notification settings may receive an email as well when you are assigned to follow up a red flag is displayed on the message you'll see a blue flag if others are assigned along with you in addition there is a follow-up filter on the overview page as well as the follow up icon at the top of the application use these to quickly see all the items assigned to you clear the flag when you have completed the assignment favorites are used to mark the items you refer to often you can mark entire conversations messages or comments within a conversation collections and even documents is a favorite most pages in the web client have a favorites filter which helps you quickly locate your most used items the favorites icon at the top of the application also provides a quick shortcut to your favorite items thank you for watching for more information be sure to visit cloud.oracle.com you
