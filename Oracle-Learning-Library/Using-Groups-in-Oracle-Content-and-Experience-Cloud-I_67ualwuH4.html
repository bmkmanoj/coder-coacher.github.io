Using Groups in Oracle Content and Experience Cloud

2017-10-05
hi and welcome an easy way to work with people in Oracle content and experience cloud is from within a group you can join groups created by others or make your own sharing folders and conversations with a group gives everyone in the group access just as if you had shared the item with each group member individually before making your own group it's a good idea to see if someone else has already added you to a similar group or if a similar group already exists that you can join note that some groups may have a private membership we mean you can see the group and add them to anything you share but you won't be able to join if there isn't a group to join that meets your needs you can make your own and add others anyone you add to your group is included when a folder or conversation is shared with the whole group be sure that when making a group you follow any guidelines your organization has such as how to name a group or who to include make the group public or private by selecting whether anyone can use the group or if they have to be a member you can also choose whether to let people add themselves useful if you're expecting the group to be very large or if a group manager has to add them which gives you more control of membership for smaller groups you can choose who can manage a group when you add them that's it once a group is created you can search for groups and share folders and conversations with them just like you do individuals quick and easy thanks for watching for more information visit cloud.oracle.com
