Working at Google Sydney - Alan: Engineering Site Director

2008-06-16
a lot of people think that if you work for Google in Australia you're going to work on Australian products for Australian users actually if you work for Google in Sydney you're going to work on global products serving users around the world we have work in three major focus areas geospatial products such as Google Maps and the associated AP is is one of the core focus areas that involves heaps of different technologies for example geospatial search data mining computer graphics client and server side architectures scalability and a whole host of really challenging technology issues a second major focus area for engineers in Sydney is online applications or collaborative applications which make up the Google Apps suite which includes Gmail and Google Calendar Google Docs the third major focus area for google city engineers is infrastructure we work on the underlying systems infrastructure that enables Google to scale its products and services to tens of millions of users around the world it's great to know that we've got a team of world-class engineers in Sydney that are contributing to products that will be used by millions of users around the world it's just a fact it's just a fantastic feeling knowing that we're doing this right here in Sydney my name is Alan Noble I'm engineering site lead for Google Sydney
