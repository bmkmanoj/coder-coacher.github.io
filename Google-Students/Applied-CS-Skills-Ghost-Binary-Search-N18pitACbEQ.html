what is a binary search binary search is a method to efficiently find an element in a sorted list of values say you have a list of all the birthdays in your class and you want to find the one that comes after yours if you turn the list into a sorted list using the month date format then you know all the birthdays in chronological order so the next date after your birthday in this list is the date you are looking for you could find your birthday in this list by looking through the list from the beginning until you find your birthday this is called a linear search and might take a long time especially if a lot of your classmates have their birthday before you a more efficient way is to use a binary search you can look at the middle of the list and based on the value there you can determine whether your birthday is in the first half for the second half now you know that you can safely ignore the other half you can then repeat this step and looking at the middle of this new list and continue until you reach your birthday once you've found your birthday you can just move along the list until you find someone with a different birthdays if you reach the end of the list then move along to the beginning of the lesson this may not seem like a lot of time saved but imagine a list of birthdays for everyone in your entire school if your school had a thousand students you may have to compare your birthday hundreds of times if you use the linear search approach by using the binary search you won't ever need more than ten comparisons to find your birthday you
