Applied CS Skills - Facilitators - Making the most of workshops

2017-05-22
hi I'm Seth and I'd like to share a few tips to help you get the most out of your applied CS workshops as you may know already applied CS units have two components the independent groundwork and the workshop groundwork is completed by student participants in their own time while workshops are the in-person sessions facilitated by you make sure you are prepared for each workshop by reading the facilitator notes watching the unit videos and familiarizing yourself with the learning objectives you'll also want to make sure you have a way to take attendance we encourage student facilitators to work with their University contacts to plan and confirm workshop location and times well in advance once the program starts ensure you and your participants have the correct time and location for the workshops at the beginning of the workshop introduce yourself take attendance and present the briefing and videos for the unit you are facilitating place students in groups and monitor them as they work through the tasks remember to take breaks as necessary or encourage students to step away for individual breaks stick to timings each week so students know what to expect encourage a collaborative environment getting students to help each other and other groups when they are stuck encourage good clean coding practice and a hands-up approach meaning it's okay to ask questions remind everyone that no one is expected to get everything right the first time if something breaks or doesn't run that's okay encourage students to be patient debug carefully and help each other out resist the urge to take control of student keyboards and complete the task for them pose questions and give hints that will bring them to the answer on their own if you notice that several students are stuck remember that you have the authority to pause explain or walk through the problem as a group everyone has different learning styles so alternate between explaining showing videos and using metaphors to ensure everyone understands and finally remember to close out the session together and remind students about where and when the next workshop will be held take time to also celebrate successes and share any problem-solving tactics you're bound to face some challenges so share them with the growing community remember there are lots of resources to make sure your workshop is a great success
