Working at Google NYC - Marcus: Engineering Director

2008-06-13
Google is kind of a unique company for me and for I think for a lot of people that work there before working at Google I worked in small companies medium-sized companies and briefly big company and in most of those cases we were very resource constrained we were encouraged to think inside a certain box how can you do more with what you've got how can you maybe take a problem that's this big and cut it down to size and make it a smaller problem at Google we're encouraged from the founders throughout the company to think big we're often challenged to think about how to make a problem bigger scale really pervades everything at Google and so for me personally that represents a challenging area whether it's trying to figure out how to grow an effective engineering organization how to operate globally or thinking about some of the technical challenges of scaling very rapidly and you know for any system that we work on we have to think in terms of what if this system were ten times bigger or a hundred times bigger a thousand times bigger and if we don't then we'll rapidly get run over millions and millions of people use Google and we hope that that will continue to be true so really the most rewarding thing for me is the opportunity to work on organizational and technical problems in the area of scale my name is Marcus Mitchell and i'm an engineering director
