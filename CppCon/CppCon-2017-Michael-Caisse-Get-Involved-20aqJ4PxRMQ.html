Last year we got an office space. We're a distributing company; we're in five different time zones. We work with a lot of different smart people and we work with a lot of really interesting problems. But one of the things that we do is we offered what we call junior engineering classes. And these junior engineering classes originally began, actually, in my house. We started teaching them 17 years ago and it was mostly our kids and their friends would show up and we'd teach everything from mechanical engineering and 3D CAD design and electronics and programming, and then we have 3D printers, so we 3D print things and we have CAD and CAM machines, and we take care of that, and throughout the years, the kids learned a lot.
