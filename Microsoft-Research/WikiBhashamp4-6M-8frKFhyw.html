hi my name is Aishwarya and I work as a researcher at Microsoft Research India i'm here to introduce wiki Pasha to you this is a new tool that allows Wikipedians to create multilingual content as you know every wikipedia article is created from scratch in every language what is nice about the stool is that it allows you to leverage the existing large repository of english wikipedia articles as you create content in your own language what this means is that you do not have to be a subject matter expert to contribute also you do not have to be familiar with the wiki format because the tool presents a simplified user interface for editing so let's see how this works I'd like to contribute to a Wikipedia article on a subject that I'm interested in the southern Indian classical dance bharata natyam so I look for the Bharata Natyam page in my local language Hindi and this is the page open here you can see that this is a stub there's not very much content on the left pane you can see a list of languages which also have bharata natyam wikipedia articles so i look for the english article and here you see a very well filled in Wikipedia article there's a lot of content which I can possibly use in creating the Hindi Wikipedia page for bharatnatyam so let's say I am interested in adding this particular piece of information from the English page the fact that Bharata Natyam as a dance is inspired by the sculptures of an ancient temple in southern India called Chidambaram you will see that after invoking wiki Pasha we are still on Wikipedia and will continue to remain there for the entire session the content creation process involves three steps the first of which is collection of relevant information to add to the page on the top left you will see the target article that we are working on in this case bharathanatyam in hindi wikipedia in the left pane is the original English Wikipedia article on Bharata Natyam which is read only in the right pane is a translation of the english wikipedia article which we can now edit notice that there is synchronized scrolling and parallel highlighting of sentences this is the sentence I want to contribute to the Hindi Wikipedia article this is machine-translated and so I may want to make a few edits before i submit it there are two ways to make edits you could type directly in the right pane when you hover the article on the other hand I could improve one of the listed suggestions by directly editing the sentence in the pop-up window once I submit this translation it immediately becomes available to all other users in the community if I wanted to add content from related Wikipedia pages I could just follow the link to the page or I could search for a particular turn and I will see a list of options editing I can now move on to step two which is composing the article in the target language in the left pane is the content that I worked on in the previous step and in the right pane is the target
